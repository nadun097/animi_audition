Free email service setup for your portfolio's CV download verification

Step 1: Create a Formspree Account

  1. Go to formspree.io
  2. Click "Sign Up" and create a free account
  3. Verify your email address

Step 2: Create a New Form

  1. After logging in, click "Create a new form"
  2. Name your form: "CV Verification"
  3. Set the form to redirect to your portfolio URL after submission
  4. Copy the form endpoint URL (it will look like: https://formspree.io/f/xxxxx)

Step 3: Configure Email Notifications

  1. In your Formspree dashboard, go to your form settings
  2. Under "Notifications", add your email address to receive submissions
  3. Enable email notifications for new submissions

Step 4: Update Your Code

Replace the placeholder in your CVVerificationModal.jsx file:

// In CVVerificationModal.jsx, find this line: const formspreeEndpoint = 'https://formspree.io/f/your_form_id' // Replace with your actual Formspree form ID: const formspreeEndpoint = 'https://formspree.io/f/abc123def' // Your actual form ID
